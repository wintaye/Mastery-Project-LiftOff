Hello, learners!

Skill Share is a communal learning platform. We believe everyone should have easy access to further education. We also believe the heart of any enterprise is people. Our mission is to facilitate education and foster community by providing the space for people to come together and share knowledge. Let's build something better.

Some Ideas We Encourage

How to use our site and what to expect

Community

Our service is meant to help build up local communities of lifelong learners, but the beauty of our interconnected world means we can build a web of communities and offer access to educational opportunities across the globe.

Education

Always wanted to learn how to crochet or build a car engine? Maybe you need a professional musician or a master woodworker teaching a class or willing to impart some wisdom. Sign up, log in, start searching.

Connection

Share your talents and get connected with others sharing theirs. Host and attend events in your area, or create online events for a further reach. We just make the platform. You make it happen!
